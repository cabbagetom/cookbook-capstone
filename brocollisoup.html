Broccoli Soup

Ingredients

  • 50g butter
  • 1 large chopped onion
  • 460g broccoli, trimmed and chopped
  • A good pinch of cumin seeds
  • 150g split red lentils
  • 1100ml vegetable stock (2 stock cubes in water)
  • 120g Stilton cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Melt the butter in a large saucepan, add the onion and cook until soft, then the broccoli and cumin and cook for about 5 min.

  2. Stir the lentils and the stock into the vegetables. Brink to the boil, then reduce the head, cover and simmer for 20-30 min (until the lentils are soft)

  3. Leave the soup to cool slightly, then puree with a hand blender (or in a processor) until smooth.

  4. If you have not used a hand blender, return the soup to the saucepain. Reheat gently,. Crumble in the Stilton, season with salt and pepper if desired, and stir until the Stilton has dissolved.

  5. If the soup is too thick add a little more water

When cold this soup can be frozen
