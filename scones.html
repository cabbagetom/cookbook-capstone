Cheese Scones

Ingredients

  • 8oz plain flour
  • 2oz butter
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 6oz grated cheese (comment from granny: I usually use Red Leicester)
  • Milk for making dough, and glazing

Instructions

  1. Rub the butter into the flour, add the baking powder, salt and cheese.

  2. Mix to a soft dough with milk.

  3. Roll out to about 1" thick, and cut into 2" triangles or circles.

  4. Put on a greased baking tray and brush with milk. Sprinkle tops with a little grated cheese

  5. Bake at 400 degrees farenheit for 10-15 minutes until golden brown

Ingredients

  • 200g plain flour
  • 55g butter
  • 15g baking powder
  • 1.5g salt
  • 170g grated cheese (comment from granny: I usually use Red Leicester)
  • Milk for making dough, and glazing

Instructions

  1. Rub the butter into the flour, add the baking powder, salt and cheese.

  2. Mix to a soft dough with milk.

  3. Roll out to about 2.5cm thick, and cut into 5cm triangles or circles.

  4. Put on a greased baking tray and brush with milk. Sprinkle tops with a little grated cheese

  5. Bake at 200 degrees centigrade for 10-15 minutes until golden brown
