Spicy Chili Recipe

IMPERIAL RECIPE

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bell peppers, chopped
  • 2 cans diced tomatoes
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat a large pot over medium-high heat. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up into small pieces with a wooden spoon.
  2. Add the onion, garlic, and bell peppers to the pot and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes, kidney beans, chili powder, cumin, salt, and cayenne pepper to the pot. Stir to combine.
  4. Bring the chili to a simmer and let cook for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the flavors have melded together.
  5. Serve hot with your favorite toppings, such as shredded cheese, sour cream, and green onions.
